The part you already know

You're not short on patients. You're short on reasons to come back.

You didn't get into this to chase rebookings. You got into it for the work — and the quiet sting of watching someone you helped drift to the clinic down the road.

01 — The work is great

Your results speak for themselves.

Patients leave happy, and they mean it when they say they'll be back. The clinical side isn't the problem.

02 — Happy isn't booked

Then life gets busy.

Without a reason and a nudge, most won't rebook until they need you again — months later, if at all.

03 — The loyalty exists

There's just nothing holding it.

No membership, no points, no reason to return on a schedule. The loyalty is real — it's slipping through the gaps between visits.
